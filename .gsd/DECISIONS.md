# DECISIONS.md — Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

# ADR-001: Use Groq (LPU) for AI Inference
- **Status**: Accepted
- **Context**: The project needs fast AI responses for WhatsApp interactions.
- **Decision**: Replace Google Gemini with Groq (Llama 3.1 8B).
- **Consequences**: Faster response times, dependency on Groq API.
